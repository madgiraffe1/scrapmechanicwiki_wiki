## Frequently Asked Questions About the Scrap Mechanic Workshop

### Q1: How do I access the Scrap Mechanic workshop?
A1: You can access the Scrap Mechanic workshop directly through the Steam client. Navigate to your Steam Library, select Scrap Mechanic, and then look for the "Workshop" tab on the game's page. Alternatively, you can search for "Scrap Mechanic workshop" on the Steam website.

### Q2: Are all items in the workshop considered mods?
A2: No, not all items are mods. The Scrap Mechanic workshop contains a mix of creations. Many are "vanilla builds" made entirely with the game's default parts, often tagged with "[No Mods]". Others are actual "mods" or "parts packs" that introduce new functionalities, items, or alter game mechanics, which may be listed under "Most Required by Other Items."

### Q3: How can I upload my own creations to the Scrap Mechanic workshop?
A3: To upload your creations, save your build in-game. Then, from the game's main menu, select "Workshop" and follow the prompts to upload your saved creation. You'll need to provide a title, description, and screenshots.

### Q4: What are the benefits of using content from the Scrap Mechanic workshop?
A4: Using content from the Scrap Mechanic workshop offers numerous benefits, including extending gameplay longevity, providing endless inspiration for your own builds, allowing you to learn advanced building techniques from others, and customizing your game experience with new parts or mechanics.
